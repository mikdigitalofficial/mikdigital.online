# Privacy Policy

- The website privacy policy is published at `privacy-policy.html` with full SEO tags (title, meta description, canonical, Open Graph, Twitter Card, JSON-LD WebSite + Breadcrumbs).
- If you update the effective date or content, also update the `sitemap.xml` `<lastmod>` for the privacy policy URL.
- If you deploy under a different domain, update canonical and all absolute URLs accordingly.
