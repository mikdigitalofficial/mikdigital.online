# MIK Digital - Complete Website Export Package

## Project Overview
Complete 4-page website for MIK Digital, a digital marketing agency specializing in Meta Ads, Google Ads, Google Business Profile optimization, and lead generation services.

## Website Structure
- **Home Page** (`index.html`) - Main landing page with hero section, services overview, and company introduction
- **Case Studies** (`case-studies.html`) - Detailed case studies and success stories
- **Services** (`services.html`) - Comprehensive services page with campaign performance data
- **Contact** (`contact.html`) - Contact information and lead generation form
